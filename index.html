About Us

Starting straight from our kitchen
Our story begins right in our own home. My son Erik and my wife Beth were baking cooking cookies in our kitchen. Erik had created a numbers of animals, but he handed me a fish cookie. "Look daddy, he said, "it's the perfect salmon cookie. It's the most delicious cookie in the world.I wish that everyone could have a cookie like this. Flash forward 5 years and we've made that dream come true. We use locally sourced and natural ingredients to bring the tastes from out kitchen to yours."

And to believe it all started out with this one cookie...
